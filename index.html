What I Do ?

I’m a final year Masters(Information Technology, Data analytics) student at Deakin university, as well as a Data analyst intern at Freelancing Hub. Right now the most important thing for me is to up-skill as fast as I can in order to play a significant role in the emerging adoption of analytics in real world. In class we are taught how to apply very methodical tools to solve business issues, but when I got out into the industry through both my internship and from meeting people in the wider industry I realized quite quickly that real life data problems are far more complex and involved than they appear. Instead of simply getting the data, crunching the numbers and then handing down a result I need to answer the bigger questions. Is the data accurate? Are the assumptions of the model aligned with the business? Will the results of our analysis answer the broader question that is being asked, or simply churn out methodically correct yet functionally useless analysis?.
